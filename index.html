Defenders of the Union
Mapping the Civil War Forts of Washington DC
During the Civil War, Washington DC was one of the most heavily fortified cities in the world. Surrounded by a ring of over sixty forts and almost one hundred batteries, the seat of the federal government devised a way to defend itself from assault by Confederate forces. This application was created to provide an immersive and interactive way to understand the defences of the national capital from both the perspective of the Civil War-era and the present day.
